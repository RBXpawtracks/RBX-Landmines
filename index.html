RBX Landmines

The RBX Landmines mod is a fun mod that adds various different landmines to defeat your enemies. Set up a trap for those evil creepers in front of your diamond fortress, or create minefields that you are putting in your next adventure server. The possibilities are endless! The landmines are pressure plates without the need of TNT!
